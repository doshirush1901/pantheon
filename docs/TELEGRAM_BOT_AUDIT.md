## Executive Summary

The Telegram bot is operational with all core systems functioning. One critical bug was found and fixed during this audit.

### Bug Fixed

**Issue:** `brain_orchestrator.py` had hardcoded collection names (`ira_chunks_voyage_v3`, `ira_emails_voyage_v3`) that don't exist, causing all document/email retrieval to silently fail with 404 errors.

**Fix:** Updated to import `COLLECTIONS` from the central `config.py` which has the correct names (`ira_chunks_v4_voyage`, `ira_emails_voyage_v2`).

**Impact:** Retrieval was broken - the bot could respond but had no access to document/email knowledge.
